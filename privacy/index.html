Privacy Policy

Equal is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Pi Network username and UID (via Pi authentication)
  • Profile information you voluntarily provide (bio, photos, interests)
  • App usage data (swipes, matches, messages)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Passwords
  • Payment information (all payments go through Pi Network)
  • Precise GPS location (only city-level)

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use PostgreSQL with SSL connections.

4. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Settings page.
